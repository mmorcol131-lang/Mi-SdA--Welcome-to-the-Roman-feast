Lectura facilitada

Welcome back to Delicious History! Today, we are going to learn about dinner parties in Ancient Rome.

Rich Romans loved having big dinner parties. They invited their friends and family to eat together in  beautiful rooms. The parties often lasted a long time.

When you eat, you probably sit on a chair. But the Romans did something different. They lay on sofas while they ate!

Romans usually ate with their hands. They enjoyed foods like eggs, fish, chicken, and meat. For dessert, they often had fruit and cake.

Some Roman foods were quite strange. One unusual food was dormice. Dormice are small animals that look a bit like mice!
